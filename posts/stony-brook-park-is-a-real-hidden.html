Stony Brook Park is a real hidden gem! 🔮

Only 20 min drive from our home and 1,5 hours from Buffalo🚗
The Park is clean and well kept. Seven dollar entrance fee unless you are sixty-two or over, or it's still winter, then it's free! 😋
Nice hike past three waterfalls in gorge with beautiful brook. Many stairs and uneven path but quite a nice one mile path. There is a beautiful natural swimming pool that we'll definetely test this summer🏞
